THE BIOMETRIC METROCARD

IT'S NOT FARE

It’s time we catch up with the evolving technology and innovation of the 21st century. Keeping track of your MetroCard or waiting in the perpetual slow-moving lines to re-load or replace a card is getting old. Riders are ready for a swipe-free world.

The question is, how can we get back those extra 30 seconds that can make the difference between making or missing a train?

THE
BIOMETRIC
METROCARD

Simply prepay for trips ID'd by your hand-print, and enter the station with a quick swipe of just your hand. Goodbye MTA MetroCard hassle.

Track Trip History

View a list of your complete and recent trip history. See how much you’ve spent from each trip, travel time, and details of every ride you’ve ever taken.

Autofill Metro Fare

Once you set up your account and link it to a credit or debit card, begin enjoying the speed and convenience of mobile fare autofill.

User-friendly

Straightforward, clean, simple interface making it super easy to access and locate different features and tools.
